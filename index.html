üîó Google Sheets Integration

Connect to Google Sheets

Import ALL sheets (customer ledgers, transactions, balance sheet) from your Google Sheets workbook

üìã Setup Instructions:

  1. Open your Google Sheets ledger workbook
  2. Click File ‚Üí Share ‚Üí Publish to web
  3. In "Link" tab, select "Entire Document"
  4. Choose ANY format (Web page or CSV - both work now!)
  5. Click Publish
  6. Copy the Spreadsheet ID from your browser URL
  7. Paste the ID or full URL below
üí° How to find Spreadsheet ID:
Your Google Sheets URL looks like:
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1abc...xyz/edit
Copy just the ID part (between /d/ and /edit) OR paste the entire URL
Example: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1abc...xyz/edit
Or just: 1abc...xyz
